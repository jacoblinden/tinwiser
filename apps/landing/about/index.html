About Us

Tinwiser is a startup at the very beginning of its journey to transform image recognition and visual intelligence.

Our Story

Born from a simple question.

What if advanced image recognition didn't require huge budgets or months of integration? What if visual data could be understood in real time — detection, tracking, and insights from any camera or feed?

That question became Tinwiser. We're a small team with backgrounds in robotics, computer vision, and machine learning — united by the belief that intelligent image recognition will become one of the most important tools across industries.

We haven't built everything yet. But we have a clear vision, a strong technical foundation, and the persistence to see it through.

What Drives Us

Our values shape our work.

Honesty

We're transparent about what we've built, what we're building, and what we don't know yet. No inflated claims.

Craft

We care deeply about the quality of what we ship. Every component, every algorithm, every interface — it matters.

Impact

We're building technology that we believe will make a real difference. That purpose guides every decision.
