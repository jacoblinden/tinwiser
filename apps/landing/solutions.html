What we're building.

Our focus areas span the full spectrum of drone-based tracking, surveillance, and aerial data collection.

Real-Time Object Tracking

AI-driven visual tracking that identifies and follows targets across complex, dynamic environments. Our systems are designed to lock on with sub-second accuracy and maintain tracking through occlusion, weather changes, and rapid movement.

Autonomous Patrol & Mapping

Pre-programmed and adaptive flight paths for continuous area surveillance and high-resolution terrain mapping. Drones that patrol, adapt to changing conditions, and build detailed spatial maps without manual control.

Multi-Spectrum Imaging

Thermal, infrared, and night-vision capabilities designed for round-the-clock operations in any lighting or weather condition. See what visible light can't reveal — from heat signatures to moisture patterns.

Live Data Streaming

Encrypted, real-time video and telemetry feeds delivered directly to command centers. Our streaming architecture is designed for minimal latency and maximum reliability, even in bandwidth-constrained environments.

Geofencing & Alerts

Define virtual perimeters and receive instant notifications when tracked objects enter or leave designated zones. Smart boundary management for security, logistics, and environmental monitoring.

Fleet Coordination

Multi-drone orchestration for large-scale operations. Synchronized coverage, intelligent task distribution, and coordinated flight patterns — managed from a single unified interface.
