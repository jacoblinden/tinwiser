What we're building.

Our focus areas span the full spectrum of image recognition — from detection and tracking to multi-spectrum imaging and visual data.

Tracking

Real-Time Object Tracking

AI-driven visual tracking that identifies and follows targets across complex, dynamic environments. We're focused on sub-second lock-on and maintaining tracking through occlusion, weather changes, and rapid movement.

Scene Understanding & Mapping

Image-based scene understanding and spatial mapping. Process video streams to build coherent views of environments, track changes over time, and surface what matters — without constant manual review.

Multi-Spectrum Imaging

Thermal, infrared, and night-vision capabilities designed for round-the-clock operations in any lighting or weather condition. See what visible light can't reveal — from heat signatures to moisture patterns.

Live Data Streaming

Real-time video and visual data streams that integrate with your systems. Architecture designed for low latency and reliability, even when bandwidth is limited.

Geofencing & Alerts

Define virtual perimeters and receive instant notifications when tracked objects enter or leave designated zones. Smart boundary management for security, logistics, and environmental monitoring.

Multi-Source Analysis

Combine and analyze feeds from multiple cameras or sources. Unified views, cross-stream tracking, and one interface to run image recognition across your setup.
